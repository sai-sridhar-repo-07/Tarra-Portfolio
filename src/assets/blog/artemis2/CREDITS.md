# Image Credits — Artemis II Blog Post

All images in this folder are sourced from NASA and are in the public domain
unless otherwise noted. NASA imagery is generally not subject to copyright.

## How to Download Official NASA Images

1. **NASA Image and Video Library**: https://images.nasa.gov/
   - Search: "Artemis II", "Orion spacecraft", "SLS rocket", "Artemis crew"

2. **NASA Artemis Press Kit Photos**: https://www.nasa.gov/press-kits/
   - Official crew portraits, mission graphics, vehicle photos

3. **NASA Flickr**: https://www.flickr.com/photos/nasahqphoto/
   - High-res mission photography released under Creative Commons
